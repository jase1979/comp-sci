GCSE Computer Science

WJEC GCSE — Units 1, 2 & 3

Unit 1: Understanding Computer Science

Written examination — 1 hour 45 minutes — 50% of qualification

  • 1. Hardware
  • 2. Logical Operations
  • 3. Communication
  • 4. Organisation & Structure of Data
  • 5. System Software
  • 6. Principles of Programming
  • 7. Software Engineering
  • 8. Program Construction
  • 9. Security & Data Management
  • 10. Ethical, Legal & Environmental Impacts

Unit 2: Computational Thinking & Programming

On-screen examination — 2 hours — 30% of qualification

  • 1. Problem Solving
  • 2. Algorithms & Programming Constructs
  • 3. Programming Languages
  • 4. Data Structures & Data Types
  • 5. Security & Authentication

Unit 3: Software Development (NEA)

Non-exam assessment — 20 hours — 20% of qualification

  • Scope of the Problem
  • Design
  • Refinement Log
  • Effectiveness of Solution
  • Technical Quality
  • Test Strategy
  • Testing
  • Further Development
